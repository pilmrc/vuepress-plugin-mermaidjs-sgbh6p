## ***Topic 2:  How to remove a WIP Serial Number from a panel***  


In the event that a 
**Board** in a **Panel** is damage or is no longer considered valid to use, the entire **Panel** does not have to be scrapped.  The systems allows one or more parts of a **Panel** to be unchecked, while other parts of the **Panel** can continue through production.  Here are the steps for such an action.  
**Click**  
to enlarge the image!![](/.attachments/29918395.png)



- **Enter**

the 
**Assembly Number** and 
**Click** on 
**Search** icon (Magnifying glass)

- **Uncheck**

the flag next to the specific serial number(s) to remove from **WIP** .
- **Click**
the **Save** icon to **Save** your changes to the **Panel** .
